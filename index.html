About the Event

A Fresh Start for Texans

Statewide Expunction Day is a free, two-day event bringing together legal aid attorneys from across Texas to help eligible residents clear their criminal records. An expunction removes an arrest or charge from your record — meaning it can no longer be seen by employers, landlords, or the public.

This event is presented by the ClearPath TX Statewide Expunction Project, a partnership of Texas legal aid organizations dedicated to making record-clearing accessible to all Texans, regardless of income.

What Is Expunction?

Expunction is a legal process that erases eligible arrests and charges from your record under Texas law (Chapter 55, Texas Code of Criminal Procedure).

Who May Be Eligible?

You may qualify if your case was dismissed, you were acquitted, or you completed deferred adjudication for certain offenses. Eligibility depends on your specific case.

Why Attend?

Get free one-on-one time with a legal aid attorney, learn if you qualify, and take the first steps toward clearing your record — at no cost to you.

Is It Really Free?

Yes. All services provided through this event are completely free for income-eligible Texans. There is no catch.
